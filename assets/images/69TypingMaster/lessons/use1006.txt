The zebras zipped by Babette as she was taking a photo on a safari in Zambia. She was amazed at their speed. There were so many of them it seemed like an exodus of zebras. Maybe that is an exaggeration, but there were zillions of them in any case. 

A fox dashed excitedly into its foxhole. Felix wondered what that was all about. After a few seconds the fox exited its domicile with extreme caution. Felix tried to coax it towards himself with a bit of ox meat but it was not interested. Felix could not think of an excuse for fox to turn down ox meat, he thought: "Something must be wrong with that fox!" 

Josie spotted an exquisite diamond ring in the window of a jewelery shop during one afternoon of shopping. She figured it must be very expensive but she went to try it on anyway. When it was on her finger she felt it was a little extravagant after all and decided to go out for a pizza at her favorite pizzeria, "@ Mario's", instead. 

Mozart visited a friend's mansion. It seemed that this friend had the deluxe version of everything. The grounds surrounding the mansion were very expansive. They reached all the way from the village down to the river. There were lots of animals living on the grounds; this Mozart found quite exciting. He wanted to know exactly what kinds of animals lived at the grounds. It was a weekend of excessive questioning for Mozart's friend.
